Third-Party Risk Assessment

A weak Supply Chain leads to cyber attacks, business interruption and data breaches. If you have technology in your environment, there is a chance that the people that make that technology themselves may be compromised, and you then may be attacked through that channel. Nearly two-thirds (64%) of companies believe the ransomware gang got into their network via one of their suppliers or business partners. (source: Cybereason Ransomware Attacks and the True Cost to Business 2022)

Organisations need to be more aware of their blind spots when it comes to supply chain risk. What permissions do these third parties have, how seriously do they take their own cyber security, what if the provider is compromised, what contractual exposure or recourse do you have? When it comes to managing this risk from your supply chain, vendors or from your technology suppliers, organisations can only truly understand this exposure through independent risk assessment.

RedBelt's specialise in assessing the exposure your vendors and supply chain partners bring to your organisation,ensuring you have a better understanding of your supply chain, interdependencies and exposures. Addressing both Threat and Defence levels gives a holistic and unparalleled insight into these exposures.

Threat Level Assessment

Measures an organisation’s risk level by revealing the entire external attack surface, reviewing and analysing threat intelligence including any unknown past breach history. RedBelt’s threat assessment is a real “eye-opener”, giving detailed insights into an organisation’s exposures.

Defence Level Assessment

Measures an organisation’s protection capability as well as their cyber risk resilience.

Redbelt’s Assessment is performed on a combined automated and manual basis importantly meaning the impact of exposures are taken into consideration.

Our Third Party assessment allows you to onboard partners with knowledge, and arms you with the information to mitigate and protect your organisation from these exposures.



Penetration Testing

RedBelts’ specialise in the Penetration Testing of Cloud Infrastructure, System, Web and Mobile application to identify vulnerabilities that could allow adversaries access to your systems and data.

Our security experts use a combination of manual and automated techniques to find weaknesses. Once identified, the pentester will attempt to exploit the vulnerabilities to see what additional access, information and privileges can be gained.
