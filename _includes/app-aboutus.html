About Red Belts

RedBelts is a specialist Cyber Security Risk Assessment and security consultant organisation. Our principal and proprietary Cyber Security Risk Assessment gives a unique insight into not only an organisation’s cyber threat level but also equally importantly, their current defence level. Our proprietary approach gives a true insight into an organisation’s cyber/privacy exposure. Accompanying our Assessment service is our market leading Virtual CISO and Penetration Testing offerings, giving organisations the capacity to master their cyber risk management, on an ongoing basis.


Unlike alternative offerings, RedBelts’ services are not fully automated meaning the substantial expertise of the Redbelt’s team is fully utilised.
